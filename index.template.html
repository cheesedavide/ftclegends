How it works

Points are awarded based of placements during the elimination rounds of the world championship, multiple placements are summed up to create a team's score. Teams that are a Captain or 1st Pick recieve double the points originally awarded. The points per placement are defined by the table below. Points don't fully reflect a team's ability as many other factors go into how a team places at worlds. To attend and then place in the elimination rounds at the world championship already proves that they are a legendary team.

Placement Points
Winning Alliance (Captain/1st Pick) 60
Finalist Alliance (Captain/1st Pick) 50
Semi-Finalist Alliance (Captain/1st Pick) 40
Quarter-Finalist Alliance (Captain/1st Pick) 30
Winning Alliance (2nd Pick) 30
Finalist Alliance (2nd Pick) 25
Semi-Finalist Alliance (2nd Pick) 20
Quarter-Finalist Alliance (2nd Pick) 15
